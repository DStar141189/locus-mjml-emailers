A leading B2B marketplace faced the risk of cutting its exponential growth over outmoded and manual process fulfillment processes that drove down the efficiency and costs of fulfillment.

The Challenges 

  • Too many delays in delivery, and high costs from manual order scheduling, sorting, and routing
  • Inefficient packing of orders in vehicles, meaning more trips and greater delivery costs
  • Too much time taken up by manual planning of order

Solutions Used 

With Locus, the e-commerce giant achieved end-to-end automation of its entire dispatch and routing operations, right from order capture to delivery. Dispatchers could now account for multiple business constraints while seamlessly generating and allocating optimized delivery routes to drivers. The main solutions implemented were:

Optimal Route Planning

The Locus Route Planning module allows companies to efficiently manage and schedule delivery routes via advanced, automated fulfillment workflows.

Automated Sorting

Locus introduced automated vehicle-based allocation to minimize shipment processing time with the help of smart route allocations for every shipment. The FMCG giant was able to optimize its last-mile efficiency, resource utilization, and transportation costs.
