Locus in Spotlight

Hi there,

We’re fast approaching the holiday season, marked by Thanksgiving, Diwali, and other festivities, and retailers are entering a critical period of heightened activity.

No shipper is a stranger to the peak season chaos, yet it’s always a surprisingly scary time for them. Large order volumes coupled with inefficient systems and policies lead to cart abandonment and Where Is My Order? (WISMO) calls—the scourge of every retail business.

In this edition, we’ll delve deep into fundamental strategies and tech solutions that can mitigate these challenges to improve customer experience, and much more. Retail leaders like JCPenney and Walmart are already reaping the benefits of these innovations, and their success offers valuable lessons for all of us.

Hope you find this insightful.
